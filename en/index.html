About the course

No gym.
No coach.
Just understanding.

The world's best runners train slowly — 80 % of the time their heart rate is low. That's not a coincidence. It's physiology.

In this course you'll learn why slow running builds the aerobic foundation, how energy moves through your muscles, what your heart rate really means, and how your body adapts week after week.

No magic. Just science — explained like a human being.

"80 percent of your training should feel too easy. That's not weakness — that's strategy."Principle of the polarised training model
